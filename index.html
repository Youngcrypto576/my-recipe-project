Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice
  • 2 tablespoonsvegetable oil
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sasame oil
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, ,minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
< class="right">

Instructions

  1. In a large pan or wok, heat vegetable oil over a medium-high heat.
  2. Add mixed vegetables to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Push vegetables to one side of the pan. Pour beaten eggs into the empty side, Scramble and cook until just set.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan. Mix everything together.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the rice and stir well.
  6. Toss in chopped green onions. Stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed.
  8. Once heated through, serve your simple and delicious fried rice immediately.
